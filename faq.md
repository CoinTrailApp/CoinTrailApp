## Integration

**CoinTrail** provides an intuitive interface to import or sync transactions from exchanges, mining pools and wallets:

- Coinbase
- CoinbasePro
- CelsiusNetwork
- CashApp
- FtxUs
- Gemini
- Kraken
- ViaBTC
- F2Pool
